Content Permissions

Public

Content marked as public will be viewable to all vistors to the site, even ones who have not registered an account.

Restricted

Content marked as restricted will only be viewable to users who have registered an account on the site.

Private

Content marked as private will only be viewable to site administrators and the user who added the content.

Note: anonymous submissions marked as Private will only be viewable to administrators.
